🌍 Our Vision

Our vision is to build a smart school assistant that helps students and teachers manage their time easily. It will use AI to create study plans, organize schedules, and make learning more effective. We want to use technology and creativity to make education smarter and more enjoyable for everyone.

🚀 Future Plans

  • Integrate AI chat across all tools for instant academic support.
  • Add smart analytics to track study progress and performance.
  • Develop mobile apps for easier access to schedules and reminders.
  • Expand to support multi-language assistance for global users.
